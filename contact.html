Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel via your itinerary page. Look for the “Cancel” button next to each item.

Can I edit my itinerary?

Yes! You can drag and drop activities or click “Edit” on any card.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use encrypted connections and secure authentication for all user data.
