Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can easily cancel your booking through your itinerary page. Simply navigate to your bookings, find the item you wish to cancel, and click the "Cancel" button. Refund policies vary based on the cancellation time and service provider terms.

Can I edit my itinerary?

Absolutely! Our platform offers flexible itinerary management. You can drag and drop activities to reorder them, click "Edit" on any card to modify details, or add new activities at any time. Changes are saved automatically.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transmission, secure authentication protocols, and comply with international data protection regulations. Your personal and payment information is always protected.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

How long does it take to get a response?

Our support team typically responds within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, you can call our 24/7 helpline. We strive to resolve all queries within 24 hours.

Do you offer group booking discounts?

Yes! We offer special discounts for group bookings of 10 or more people. Contact our sales team for customized packages and corporate travel solutions. Early bird discounts are also available for advance bookings.
